FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a typical project cost?

+
Project costs vary widely based on scope, complexity, and timeline. A simple website might start at $500, while a full SaaS platform can range from $5,000 to $50,000+. We provide detailed, transparent quotes after a free discovery call.

How long does development take?

+
A landing page can be delivered in 1–2 weeks. A mobile app typically takes 8–16 weeks. Enterprise platforms vary. We always give you a realistic timeline upfront and keep you updated throughout.

Do you provide post-launch support?

+
Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing maintenance, feature development, and technical support. We consider ourselves your long-term technology partner, not just a one-off vendor.

Can you work with our existing codebase?

+
Absolutely. We frequently audit and extend existing systems. We'll start with a code review and provide an honest assessment before committing to any work.

Do you sign NDAs?

+
Yes, we sign NDAs for all client engagements. Your business ideas, source code, and data are fully confidential. We also transfer full IP rights upon project completion.
